================================================================
SCHEDULE 2 — SERVICE SCOPE
================================================================

Section 3.1 Base Package

The Supplier shall provide the following services as part of the Base
Package for the fixed annual fee set out in Schedule 4:

  (a) Level 2 incident management for the SP7 platform during business
      hours (Mon–Fri, 08:00–18:00 CET);
  (b) Monthly availability reporting;
  (c) Configuration management of the production environment, including
      but not limited to such operational adjustments as the Customer
      may reasonably request;
  (d) Warranty support for any defect identified during the Warranty
      Period, with no separate fee and no cap on remediation hours.

Section 3.2 Time & Materials Add-Ons

The following services are available on a Time & Materials basis at the
rates set out in the Rate Card (Schedule 5):

  - Out-of-hours incident response (Rate Card item RC-OOH-01)
  - Custom report development (Rate Card item RC-RPT-04)
  - Migration and major version upgrades (Rate Card item RC-MIG-02)

================================================================
SCHEDULE 3 — PATCH MANAGEMENT
================================================================

Section 4.1 The Supplier shall apply security patches classified as
"Critical" or "High" by the vendor to all SP7 production servers within
14 calendar days of release. Routine OS and application patches shall be
applied on a quarterly basis.

Section 4.2 Patch management is included in the Base Package fee for
the term of this Agreement and is not separately chargeable.

Section 4.3 Patch management services under this Schedule terminate on
31 December 2027, after which patch management shall be available only
on a Time & Materials basis.

================================================================
SCHEDULE 6 — SERVICE LEVELS
================================================================

Section 7.2 Service-level credits payable by the Supplier in respect of
any failure to meet the agreed availability target shall be capped at
fifteen percent (15%) of the annual Base Package fee.
